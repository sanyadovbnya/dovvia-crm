Common questions

How long does setup take? ▾

24-48 hours. I'll port your existing number (or give you a new one), train Max on your shop, and walk you through everything in a 30-minute call.

Do I have to change my phone number? ▾

No. Keep your existing number. We forward calls to Max only when you don't pick up — or you can fully route to AI and have both options.

What languages does Max speak? ▾

English natively. Spanish and Russian are in development.

What if Max gets confused or can't help? ▾

Max captures the contact and marks the call as "waiting on callback." You call them back when convenient — they already know you got their request.

What if I don't like it? ▾

Cancel anytime through the customer portal. Setup fee is non-refundable once activated (it covers 4-6 hours of dedicated configuration work).

Can I send only some calls to AI? ▾

Yes. Set up your phone provider so AI only answers when you're busy or after-hours. Hybrid mode works great.
